## What Is Stitch?

**Stitch** is a cloud-native, fully managed **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) data integration platform** that automatically moves data from a wide variety of source systems into a centralized data warehouse — such as **Google BigQuery**, Amazon Redshift, or Snowflake — where it can be analyzed and acted upon.

Rather than relying on manual data exports, custom scripts, or overworked engineering teams to keep data pipelines running, Stitch handles all of that automatically, reliably, and at scale.

---

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Automated Data Integration
**Stitch eliminates the need for manual ETL scripting.** Once configured, data flows from source systems to your data warehouse automatically — on a schedule or in near real-time — with no ongoing engineering intervention required. This frees up technical resources for higher-value work.

### 2. 130+ Pre-Built Connectors
Stitch supports connections to a vast library of popular business tools and platforms, including:

- **CRM & Marketing:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Google Analytics, Google Ads
- **Finance & Commerce:** Stripe, QuickBooks, Shopify, NetSuite
- **Databases:** MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Microsoft SQL Server
- **Productivity & Collaboration:** Google Sheets, Zendesk, Jira, Slack

This means **our existing tools can all feed into a single, unified data environment** — without building custom integrations from scratch.

### 3. Real-Time Data Syncing
Stitch supports **continuous, near real-time data replication**, ensuring that analytics dashboards and reports always reflect the latest business data. This dramatically reduces the lag between when something happens in the business and when decision-makers can see it.

### 4. Reduced Manual Data Work
Today, data teams often spend a disproportionate amount of time maintaining brittle pipelines, fixing broken exports, and manually reconciling data from different sources. **Stitch automates this entire layer**, reducing errors, saving time, and improving confidence in the data.

### 5. Improved Analytics Readiness
By delivering clean, structured, and consistently refreshed data into a centralized warehouse, Stitch ensures that **analytics and BI tools (such as Google Looker, Tableau, or Power BI) always have reliable data to work with** — enabling faster, more accurate reporting and decision-making at every level of the organization.

### 6. Scalability Without Complexity
Stitch is designed to **scale with business growth** — handling increasing data volumes, new integrations, and additional warehouses without requiring infrastructure management. Its transparent, row-based pricing model makes cost predictable as usage grows.
